Pipits Cottage in Exmoor

Pipits is a privately owned family cottage that sleeps 8. Two double rooms, a bunk bedded room with a single bed and a sofa bed in one of the sitting rooms. There are two bathrooms upstairs. This cottage is situated in the heart of Exmoor, in the tranquil village of Exford. There are two pubs within walking distance. Fantastic walks with beautiful scenery. The nearest beach is 50 minutes away. Stables are available in the village for your own horse. Superb rides on the moor. Travel time from Banbury is roughly 3 hours. Change over day is Saturday. This cottage has been offered for 1 week. We will provide all linen and towels. The kitchen is well equipped.
